About Us

Welcome to Slab Stash, your friendly little corner for affordable coin collecting!

Our Story

Slab Stash began with a simple thought: coin collecting should be easy on the wallet and big on fun. We’re passionate about slabbed coins—those neatly graded gems—and love offering them at prices that don’t require a treasure map to afford. It’s just us, bringing you coins we’d happily keep ourselves.

What We Do

We sell a handpicked stash of slabbed coins at an affordable price point, straight from us to you. No complications, just quality coins that fit your budget and your collection. We’re also working on a buyback program (details TBD)—because we think collecting should be a two-way street.

Our Mission

To make coin collecting simple, affordable, and enjoyable, creating a warm spot for folks who share our love for slabbed coins.

Why Choose Slab Stash?

  • Straight from Us: Every coin comes directly from Slab Stash, plain and simple.
  • Affordable Prices: We keep it budget-friendly, so you can collect without the stress.
  • Friendly Vibes: We’re a small team who’d rather share a smile than a sales pitch.
  • Slabbed Coin Focus: We’re all about those polished, graded coins that make collecting special.
  • Integrity First: Every coin is authentic and graded right—no funny business here.
