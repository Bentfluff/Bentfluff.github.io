Privacy Policy

At Slab-Stash, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and shipping address when you place an order or sign up for our mailing list.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and time spent, collected via cookies and tracking technologies.
  • Payment Information: We do not store payment details directly; these are handled securely by our payment processor, Stripe.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders for our mystery coin packs.
  • To send you updates, promotional offers, and newsletters (you may opt-out at any time).
  • To improve our website and services based on user behavior and feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except:

  • To trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you (e.g., shipping providers), provided they agree to keep your information confidential.
  • When required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Slab-Stash and its users.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including:

  • Secure server hosting with encryption for data transmission.
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security audits and updates to our systems.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us at customersupport@slab-stash.com.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. We may also use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve our services, but we do not share identifiable data with these tools.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant updates via email or a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of Slab-Stash after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
